HOW TO PLAY

The goal of the game is to find as many bananas as possible around the circle!
For reference, the rightmost part of the circle is 0 degrees, and the topmost part of the circle is 90 degrees.

The angle which holds the most bananas will be indicated by:
Bananas located at:
degrees

To lock in your guess, click "Search!"

The number of bananas you collect are represented by this table:
0° away = 10 1° away = 8 2° away = 6 3° away = 4 4° away = 2 5° away = 1

After 5 attempts, your total collected bananas will represent your score!
